INTRO

My research is focused on the evolution of circumstellar discs, planet formation and planetary system evolution, with two primary tranches; detailed studies of disc substructures to investigate planet-disc interactions (in discs around both pre- and main sequence stars), and demographic surveys of young stellar objects (YSOs) to measure the mass budgets and structures of discs at the dawn of giant planet and terrestrial planet formation. I primarily work with (sub)-millimeter, radio, & mid-/far-infrared continuum & spectral line observations/simulations (ALMA, SMA, VLA, JWST), and on multi-wavelength SED modelling.

I am involved in a number of ongoing projects analysing stellar and disc demographics in young star forming regions, modelling the morphology of debris discs, the large ALMA programme, ARKS see Cycle 9 projects here and the Extended Westerlund 1 and 2 Open Clusters Survey, EWOCS.
